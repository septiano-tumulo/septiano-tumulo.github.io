Surga

Before Jehovah's Awful Throne

1. Before Jehovah's awful throne, Ye nations,
bow with sacred joy; Know that the Lord
is God alone; He can create, and He destroy.

2. His sovereign power, without our aid, Made us of
clay, and formed us men; And when like wandering
sheep we strayed, He brought us to His fold again.

3. We'll crowd His gates with thankful songs, high as the
heavens our voices raise; And earth, with her ten
thousand tongues, Shall fill His courts with sounding praise.

4. Wide as the world is His command, vast as Etruth
shall stand, When rolling years shall cease to move.

Di Hadapan Hadirat Hu

1. Dihadapan hadirat Hu, Hai bangsa-
bangsa, tunduklah; ketahuilah
olehmu, Khalikmu itulah Allah.

2. Dari lebu dan tanahlah, Kita dijadikan
Tuhan; Dan bila tersesat
kita, Tuhan tak akan tinggalkan.

3. Kuasa dan kasih Allah, Memenuhi
segenap dunia; tetap, teguhlah
firman-Nya, Hingga penuh hadirat-Nya

4. Di pintu surga yang suci, Menyanyi
beribu lidah; Tuhan kita akan
puji, Sampai selama-lamanya

Praise God From Whom All Blessings Flow

Praise God, from Whom all blessings flow;
Praise Him, all creatures here below;
Praise Him above, ye heavenly host;
Praise Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
